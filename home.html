Welcome to the official tourism website of Rome, the Eternal City—your ultimate guide to discovering the rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landmarks of one of the world’s most captivating destinations. Whether you're planning your first visit or returning to explore more of Rome's timeless beauty, this website provides everything you need to make your trip unforgettable.

What You'll Find: Explore Iconic Landmarks: From the awe-inspiring Colosseum and the ancient Roman Forum to the majestic St. Peter’s Basilica and the world-famous Pantheon, uncover the stories behind Rome’s most iconic monuments. Detailed guides and interactive maps make it easy to navigate the city’s historical gems.

Hidden Gems: Dive deeper into Rome’s lesser-known attractions—charming piazzas, peaceful gardens, and underground catacombs—offering unique experiences that showcase the city’s beauty beyond the crowds.

Event Calendar: Stay updated with the latest events, festivals, and exhibitions happening throughout the year. Whether it’s a religious celebration, a cultural festival, or a temporary art exhibition at the Vatican Museums, you’ll never miss out on the vibrant happenings of Rome.

Guided Tours & Experiences: Discover a range of tours tailored to your interests. From guided walking tours through ancient ruins to bike rides through the picturesque streets, find the perfect way to experience Rome like never before.

Practical Information: Get all the essential travel info—directions, public transport tips, opening hours for popular attractions, ticket prices, and insider tips to help you navigate the city with ease.

Local Cuisine: No trip to Rome is complete without tasting its world-renowned food. Explore the best restaurants, cafés, and gelaterias in the city, from traditional trattorias to Michelin-starred dining experiences.

Shop & Stay: Find the best places to stay, from boutique hotels to luxurious resorts, and shop for authentic Roman souvenirs, fashion, and handcrafted goods.

Travel Tips & Visitor Services: Helpful tips to make your visit smoother, from language basics to safety advice, and links to visitor centers offering brochures, maps, and personalized assistance.

Whether you’re an art lover, history enthusiast, foodie, or simply someone who wants to experience Rome’s unique charm, this website is your one-stop resource for planning your visit. Let Rome’s beauty, history, and vibrant atmosphere captivate you—one click at a time!
